## Reviewer-orientation note (what a checklist will and won't find cleanly)

- **Clearly documented (expect MET):** symptom duration (~11 months), objective neurological
  deficit (diminished patellar reflex, EHL 4/5, L5 sensory loss), imaging-symptom
  correlation (MRI L4-L5 severe right lateral recess stenosis matching right L5 radiculopathy),
  documented instability (flexion/extension X-ray + Grade I spondylolisthesis), absence of
  active-smoking contraindication (former smoker, quit 7 years ago), single-level surgery.
- **At risk of reading as NOT DOCUMENTED:** *minimum continuous duration of conservative
  therapy.* The injection (page 90) and PT (pages 41–44) and medications (page 14) are all
  present but scattered, and the page that would confirm PT met the policy minimum continuous
  duration is a degraded handwritten fax (page 43, OCR flagged). A reviewer who does not
  assemble pages 14 + 41–44 + 90 may score conservative-therapy duration as not documented —
  exactly the variance Summit Ridge measured.
