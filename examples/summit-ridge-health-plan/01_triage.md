- **Named pain with a volume or time cost:** Present, multiple. Complex file end-to-end:
  *"Three to four hours."* Appeals: *"about a six-week appeals backlog... roughly 180 open

- **Decision made against written criteria:** Present — *highest-value signal, fired hard.*
  *"We have written criteria for all of this... we've layered our own coverage policy on top —
  Summit Ridge has its own medical policy documents... In theory it's a checklist. The record
  either meets the criteria or it doesn't."* Paired with measured variance: *"Seventeen
  percent. One in six complex cases... came to different answers."* And much of the variance is
  document-comprehension, not medicine: *"It wasn't a disagreement about medicine. It was a
  disagreement about what was in the file."*
- **Buried workflow mention (training/support aside):** Present, secondary — onboarding pain
  surfaced as an aside: *"onboarding takes months precisely because so much of the skill is
  'knowing where to look and what our policy actually requires.'"* And the unanswerable-precedent
